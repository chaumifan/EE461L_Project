    About Pocket Recipes

    Have you ever had the problem where you have a full fridge but don't know what to do with all your ingredients?


    Simply enter in all of the ingredients you want to use and we will curate a list of recipes that fit your fridge.


    We're just a group of students who love food as much as you do. Unfortunately, we were never really good cooks. We needed your help, and so we actually build this site just to help ourselves become better cooks.

    It's as easy as 1, 2, 3!

    1. Type an ingredient you want included in the your personalized list of recipes in the TOP box. Hit Enter and repeat for all ingredients.


    2. Type an ingredient you are allergic to or just don't like in the BOTTOM box. Hit Enter and repeat for all ingredients


    3. Browse your perfectly curated list of recipes!
